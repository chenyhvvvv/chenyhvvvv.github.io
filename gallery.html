Chicago

Chicago is close to my school, just a two-hour bus ride away, so my good friend Ruoping and I often go there for sightseeing and shopping. Chicago's night view is the most beautiful among all the cities I've visited, bright and bustling. The wind here is extremely strong in winter, and we all shiver from the cold.
